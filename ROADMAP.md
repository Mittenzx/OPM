## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks
- **Engine updates breaking plugin**
  - Mitigation: Version compatibility matrix, early access testing
  
- **Performance issues with large operations**
  - Mitigation: Profiling, optimization, background processing

### Business Risks
- **Market saturation**
  - Mitigation: Focus on quality, unique features, community
  
- **Pricing competition**
  - Mitigation: Value-based pricing, superior documentation

### Community Risks
- **Negative reviews**
  - Mitigation: Responsive support, rapid bug fixes, clear expectations
  
- **Feature requests overwhelming development**
  - Mitigation: Clear roadmap, prioritization framework, MVP focus
